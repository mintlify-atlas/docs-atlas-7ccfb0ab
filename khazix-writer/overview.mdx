</Info>

## What It Does

The `khazix-writer` skill helps you write WeChat long-form articles (公众号长文) in Khazix's personal voice. Khazix is a content creator and entrepreneur who has spent three years in the AI industry, running the public account 「数字生命卡兹克」.

The author's voice in one sentence:

> **"有见识的普通人在认真聊一件打动他的事。"**
> A knowledgeable ordinary person earnestly discussing something that moved them.

### When It Activates

This skill triggers when the user needs to write a long-form article for WeChat. Trigger phrases include:

- 写文章、写稿子、帮我写、续写、扩写
- 公众号文章、长文、出稿
- 按我的风格写
- "帮我把这个写成文章"
- "用我的风格写一下"

It also activates when the user provides any source material — a PDF, brief, news link, voice-to-text transcript, or scattered notes — and says "帮我写篇文章."

### What It Does NOT Handle

<Warning>
  Do **not** use this skill for:
  - Short content: Xiaohongshu posts, Twitter/X threads, WeChat Moments
  - Pure title or summary generation (use the `wechat-title` skill for that)
</Warning>

## Core Values

These values form the foundation of every article. They are not stylistic choices — they are the author's genuine beliefs.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="永远对世界保持好奇" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Face every new tool and technology not with anxiety ("will I be replaced?") but with excitement ("what can I make with this?"). This is the account's slogan and the starting point of all content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="讲人话，像个活人" icon="person">
    "Human presence" (活人感) is the scarcest thing in the AI era. Share personal experiences, real feelings, and mistakes made. Use "我觉得" and "我认为" freely. Embrace imperfection.
  </Card>
  <Card title="真诚是唯一的捷径" icon="heart">
    You can choose not to write about something, but never deceive. If a product has flaws, say so. If you don't know something, admit it openly. Reader trust is the most valuable asset.
  </Card>
  <Card title="有所为有所不为" icon="shield">
    Do not chase traffic that conflicts with your values. Before writing, ask: is this something I genuinely believe and want to express?
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## The Five Article Archetypes

Every Khazix article belongs to one of five archetypes. Identifying the archetype before writing determines the structural approach, emphasis, and tone.

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="1. Investigative-Experiment Type（调查实验型）" icon="flask">
    Go do the thing yourself, then report back. *Buy the ¥9.9 DeepSeek. Poison an AI with fake notes. Hire someone to install OpenClaw.* Core premise: "I did this so you don't have to." Writing focuses on process narrative and layered discovery.
  </Card>
  <Card title="2. Product-Experience Type（产品体验型）" icon="mobile">
    Get the product, use it for real, bring the reader along for the ride. Not a review report — more like "come play with me." Writing focuses on scene demonstration and authentic reactions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="3. Phenomenon-Analysis Type（现象解读型）" icon="eye">
    Spot an interesting phenomenon, then dig deeper layer by layer. *Why did that three-panel AI image go viral? Why can't AI see the heart shape?* Core: "Did you notice this? What's behind it?" Writing follows: observation → curiosity → research → philosophical elevation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="4. Tool-Sharing Type（工具分享型）" icon="wrench">
    Share a useful tool or Prompt, but wrap it in a personal story. Never open with "I recommend a great tool." Writing focuses on personal story setup → natural tool introduction → usage process → wow-factor result.
  </Card>
  <Card title="5. Methodology-Sharing Type（方法论分享型）" icon="book">
    Systematically share long-accumulated experience and methodology. Core: "I'm handing you my best stuff." Writing must include actionable steps for every section, honest acknowledgment of learning curves and failure points, a humility preface to defuse any "let me teach you" arrogance, and a closing that returns to all action points and elevates.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
