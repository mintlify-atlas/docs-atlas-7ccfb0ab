## What is Khazix Skills?

Khazix Skills is an open-source collection of AI Skills published by Khazix (数字生命卡兹克). Each skill follows the [Agent Skills](https://agentskills.io) open standard — a structured way to package domain expertise into reusable modules that AI agents can load on demand.

These are the skills Khazix actually uses, refined over time through real-world content creation and shipped here exactly as-is, without modifications.

## About the author

Khazix (数字生命卡兹克) is an AI content creator and entrepreneur with three years of experience in the AI industry. He runs the WeChat public account 「数字生命卡兹克」, driven by the mission to spark curiosity about AI in everyone who reads his work.

## Design philosophy

Every skill in this collection is built around three principles:

- **Composable** — multiple skills can work together in a single workflow
- **Portable** — the same skill works across different AI tools and agents
- **On-demand loading** — agents load skills only when the context calls for it, keeping things lightweight
