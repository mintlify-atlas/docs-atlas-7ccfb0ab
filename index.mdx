## Get started in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a skill">
    Browse the available skills. Start with **khazix-writer** for WeChat long-form content creation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install via your agent">
    In Claude Code, Codex, or OpenClaw, say: `安装这个 skill：https://github.com/KKKKhazix/khazix-skills`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Trigger the skill">
    Ask your agent to write an article. The skill activates automatically when you mention writing, drafting, or WeChat articles — or call it directly with `/khazix-writer`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review with the quality checklist">
    After generation, run through the four-layer self-check (L1–L4) to ensure the article passes all style and quality gates.
  </Step>
</Steps>
